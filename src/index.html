About

Bertolini Studio is a cutting-edge architectural design firm founded in 2015. We specialize in creating innovative, sustainable spaces that seamlessly blend form and function. Our approach combines traditional architectural principles with modern design technologies.

Our Team

Led by Marco Bertolini, our studio brings together a diverse team of architects, designers, and creative thinkers. With over 20 years of collective experience, we've successfully delivered projects ranging from intimate residential spaces to large-scale commercial developments.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the power of thoughtful design to transform lives and communities. Every project is an opportunity to create spaces that inspire, function flawlessly, and stand the test of time. Sustainability and innovation are at the core of everything we do.
